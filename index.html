Introduction

BanG Dream!, a new generation girls band project where anime, games, comics and live performances all come together! The franchise features 7 unique bands with a total of 35 characters, all with different personalities, looks and stories. The game features over 100 original songs as well as covers from popular songs like: Cruel Angel Thesis, Silhouette, Guren no Yumiya, A Tale of Six Trillion Years and a Night, Don't say "lazy" and many more! Enjoy fully voiced character, band and event stories with high quality animated live2D members and immerse yourself in each band's exciting journey towards stardom through their individual band stories.
